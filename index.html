What's Unique About Lotto Chain?

Lotto Chain is a blockchain-based lotto & lottery platform that offers a transparent, secure and trusted gaming experience. Using decentralized technology, Lotto Chain ensures that every transaction, bet placed and payment is permanently recorded on the blockchain and cannot be manipulated.All lottery results are taken directly from official sources that are internationally recognized, which are already trusted.

How Does Lotto Chain Work?

Select Market & Bet

Players choose the desired market (Hong Kong, Singapore, Sydney, China, Cambodia, or Bull Eye) and place a bet.

Official Results

The draw results are taken directly from the official sources of each market, ensuring full transparency.

Blockchain Security

All transactions are recorded in the blockchain, decentralized, ensuring no manipulation or alteration of data.

Anonymity & Privacy

Players can participate without having to reveal personal identity, and without KYC, player privacy remains intact.

Fast & Efficient

Without the involvement of a third party, transactions are fast and costs are lower than conventional lotteries.

No Initial Deposit

Lotto Chain allows you to place bets without any initial deposit. Simply use the MetaMask wallet app, and you can start betting right away.
